Meet Hotey!

About Hotey

Hotey is our mini Sicilian donkey. He was living in a dirt field with his alpaca friend up until some wildfires threatened his home and the family who needed to rehome him asked us to come and get him. He's 13 years old and just loves to have a cuddle, a nose scratch, and a carrot.
